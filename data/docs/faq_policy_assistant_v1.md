# Policy Assistant FAQ (v1.0)

## What if the assistant is unsure?
If it cannot find evidence in provided documents, it should say:
"I don’t know based on the available policy set."

## How are answers validated?
Answers must cite policy sections. If two documents conflict, show both.

## Can I upload new documents?
Only if they are approved as Internal or Public. Restricted documents require explicit approval.
