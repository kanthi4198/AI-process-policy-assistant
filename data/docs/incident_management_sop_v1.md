# Incident Management SOP (v1.0)

## Severity levels
- **Sev 1**: customer-facing outage, safety risk, or major data exposure.
- **Sev 2**: degraded service with workaround.
- **Sev 3**: minor issue, low impact.

## Escalation criteria (must escalate immediately)
- suspected data breach
- safety-related malfunction
- repeated failures in a critical workflow
- inability to restore within 30 minutes for key services

## Escalation path
1. Notify on-call incident manager
2. Create incident ticket with timeline
3. Engage Security Office if data exposure suspected
4. Post-incident review within 5 business days
